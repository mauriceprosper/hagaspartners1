About Our Company

Bringing Ideas & Innovation to Life

At Hagas and Partners, we're your premier destination for superior ceiling and wall finishing, renovation works, and construction project management.

With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to excellence, we transform spaces into breathtaking works of art. Our dedicated team ensures every project is executed with precision, creativity, and a relentless pursuit of client satisfaction.

Trust us to bring your vision to life and exceed your expectations every step of the way.

Our Services

Ceiling Installation

Armstrong, Gypsum Board, POP, Luxalon Ceiling and more — precision-fitted ceiling systems for any space.

Wall Cladding

Decorative and functional wall cladding solutions that add character, texture, and lasting value to your interiors.

Floor Finishes

Premium floor finishing services including tiling, screeding, stamped concrete, and polished finishes.

Dry Wall Partition

Versatile, fast-install drywall partition systems for offices, homes, and commercial buildings.

Glass Partition

Elegant glass partition systems that create open, light-filled spaces while maintaining privacy.

General Renovation

Complete residential and commercial renovation works — from concept to completion with zero compromise on quality.

Heat Thermal Insulation

Advanced thermal insulation solutions that improve energy efficiency and comfort across all climates.

Wall Finishes

Expert screeding, painting, texturing, and decorative finishes that transform bare walls into polished surfaces.

Construction Project Management

End-to-end project management — planning, procurement, scheduling, and delivery supervision — so your project runs on time and on budget.
